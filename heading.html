HTML Headings:

HTML Headings are titles or subtitles that we want to display
on a webpage.

HTML headings are defined with the <h1> to <h6> tags.
<h1> defines the most important heading.
<h6> defines the least important heading.

Example:

<h1> Heading 1 </h1>
<h2> Heading 2 </h2>
<h3> Heading 3 </h3>
<h4> Heading 4 </h4>
<h5> Heading 5 </h5>
<h6> Heading 6 </h6>

Output:

Heading 1

Heading 2

Heading 3

Heading 4

Heading 5
Heading 6
